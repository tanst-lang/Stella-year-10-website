Tips and tricks, intermediate level

here are some usefull tips to help take your minecraft skills to the next level. seperate sections for survival and creative*.

*Can be used the other way around if wanted.


Tips and tricks for creative mode

  • If you need to count how many blocks there are in a line, rather than counting each block follow these simple steps.
    1. turn on your coordinates.
    2. go to one end of your line of blocks and take note of your coordinates.
    3. travel to the other end of your line of blocks and note your new coordinates
    4. the difference in your coordinates are how many blocks there are in the line.

  • Layer different banner patterns to make your own super unique design.

  • You can place multpile tree saplings next to each other then bonemeal one of them to create one giant tree!

  • If you want to spawn in a specific type of villager, randomly spawning them in will take a long time and possibly won't even work. Villager variations have specific blocks that trigger them to become the variation, these blocks are commonly known as profession blocks or job blocks. By spwaning a villager near a profession block it will automatically get assigned that profession. Click here for each villager proffesion and its corresponding profession block +more

Tips and tricks for survival

  • If you have an animal farm and are sick of the animals escaping when you open the gate, simply put carpet on top of one or more of the fence blocks. You will be able to jump on top of it to get in and out but mobs won't.

  • rather than making chests to store your items try using barrels instead. Chests require 8 wooden planks to craft while barrels only require 6 wooden planks and two wooden slabs. This means that for the same amount of wood that creates 7 chests you can make 8 barrels. chests also cannot open if another block is ontop of it (exept for a few exeptions) but barrels can. Barrels also provide a more discrete look as you could have a floor made of barrels and no one would bat an eye whereas chests are much more obvious.

  • if you are strip mining and gravel starts falling infront of you, break the bottom one and quickly place a torch in its place. You will have to do this quickly, but if done correctly the pile of gravel will fall onto the torch and break, creating an almost waterfall like effect

  • use old wooden tools as fuel for your furnace. That's right you can use old wooden tools as emergency fuel for your furnace, just pop it in where your regular fuel would go.

  • on the topic of furnaces did you know you can use a campfire to cook your food instead? simply get your campfire and place your desired food on it, you can even cook up to four items at a time!

  • If you are stranded with no food but you do have flint and steel, rather than killing animals like normal to get food, light them on fire. Doing this will cause the mob to drop cooked food rather than raw food.
